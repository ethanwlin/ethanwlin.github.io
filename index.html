Big 10 Football Chant Lyric Compositions

Let's explore the lyric composition for each school chant in the Big 10 Football Conference.

Authors Ethan Lin, Joshua Wildermuth
Published Jan. 20, 2026

Overview and Dataset

Our dashboard aims to analyze the frequency of different phrases across chants in the Big 10 Football Conference while showing each chant's distribution across BPM's and chant durations.

Dataset Information: Our dataset was provided as a csv file containing a variety of information including school name, BPM, chant duration, and whether or not each chant contained certain phrases such as "Victory" or "Rah".

We utilized external online data by researching the song lyrics and compiling how many times a phrase was in a chant.
